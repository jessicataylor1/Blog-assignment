IXXN 221: Web Design - Jessica Taylor

About

I'm a second year design innovation student at Victoria University of Wellington. I'm majoring in Interaction Design and minoring in Communications Design. I didn't always see myself going into design but in my last year of high school I made the decision to come to Victoria's School of Design and now I don't see myself doing anything else. After university I plan to move to Christchurch and hopefully get a job in UX design. I look up to the likes of David Carson, IDN magazine, Grace Owen, Hattie Stewart and Henn Kim.
